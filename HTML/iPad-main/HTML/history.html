Journey

It has started in 1916, in Munih, Germany. It has been developing since 1916, with improved technologies, car dynamics. and appereances.

BMW is the brand name for Bayerische Motoren Werke GmbH. Rapp-Motorenwerke of Munich was turned into BMW in 1917. The company was first established as Knorr-Bremse AG in 1920 before being refounded as BMW AG in 1922. It was the successor of the Bayerische Flugzeugwerke AG, established in 1916. BMW's founding year is thus considered to have been 1916.
